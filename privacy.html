Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Content_farm is a personal, single-user desktop application. It has no third-party users, so it collects no personal information from anyone other than its author.

  1. What is collected. Nothing. The application does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data from third parties. It has no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking.
  2. Authentication tokens. To post to the author's own social media accounts, the application stores OAuth access tokens for those accounts locally on the author's computer. These tokens are never transmitted anywhere except to the issuing platform's official API.
  3. Platform data. The only account data read from a platform is the minimum needed to confirm which account is authenticated (e.g. TikTok's user.info.basic scope). It is not stored beyond the local token files or shared with anyone.
  4. Contact. Privacy questions can be sent to the author at parthivkhaund@gmail.com.
