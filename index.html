Content_farm

Content_farm is a personal desktop tool that produces and publishes original short-form motivational videos — stoic philosophy quotes and reflections — to its author's own social media accounts (YouTube Shorts, and TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API).

It is a single-user application: it runs on its author's computer, posts only to accounts owned by its author, and has no third-party users, no sign-up, and no hosted service.
